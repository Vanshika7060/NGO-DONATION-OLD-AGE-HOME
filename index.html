ABOUT US:

We are group of people running a old age home in chandigarh for senior citizens who are homeless and need emotional support,recreation facilites to overcome social isolation. most people live abroad and their ageing parents are left alone and they have no one to take care of them
this place is like a home for them where they get everything life food,clothes,shelterand medical treatment.
